Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the GodGPT Affiliate Program? expand_more
Anyone can join! Whether you're a content creator, wellness coach, spiritual guide, or simply someone who loves GodGPT, you're welcome. Sign up and start sharing immediately.
How much can I earn? expand_more
You earn 30% recurring commission on every subscription plan: Weekly, Monthly, and Annual. Your earnings grow with each referral and continue as long as they stay subscribed. There are no caps or limits. Top affiliates earn hundreds to thousands per month.
What is eligible for a commission or reward? expand_more
You earn commission on all paid GodGPT subscriptions: Weekly, Monthly, and Annual plans. Once someone signs up through your unique affiliate link and subscribes to any plan, you receive 30% of their subscription payment every billing cycle. Free trials or free tier users don't generate commissions until they convert to paid plans.
Do I need to be a GodGPT user or customer? expand_more
While it's not required, we highly recommend using GodGPT yourself. Your authentic experience and personal insights will make your promotion more genuine and effective. Many successful affiliates share their own transformation stories, which resonates deeply with potential users.
Are there any restrictions on how I can promote? expand_more
You're free to promote GodGPT through social media, blogs, YouTube, podcasts, newsletters, and personal recommendations. We encourage authentic, honest promotion that highlights real value.
How is GodGPT different from other AI tools? expand_more
GodGPT is purpose built for spiritual growth, self reflection, and emotional clarity, not productivity or task completion. While other AI tools help you work faster, GodGPT helps you understand yourself deeper. It's designed for meaningful conversations about life, purpose, and inner peace. This unique positioning makes it easy to promote alongside (not against) other AI tools your audience may already use.
Who can I contact for GodGPT Affiliate support? expand_more
For support and general inquiries regarding your GodGPT affiliate partnership, you can reach out to our dedicated affiliate support team via email at info@godgpt.com.
