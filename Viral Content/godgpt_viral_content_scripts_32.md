#### 1. **Emotional Hook Optimization**
**What Works:** Relatable pain points, direct "you" language, curiosity gaps
**Applied To All Scripts:**
- Changed from: "Here's how to manifest money"
- Changed to: "You've been trying to manifest money for months. Nothing works. Here's why..."

#### 2. **Story Arc Structure (Problem ‚Üí Insight ‚Üí Solution)**
**What Works:** Address struggle ‚Üí Reveal why ‚Üí Show GodGPT solution
**Applied To All Scripts:**
- Problem (3-5s): Specific struggle
- Insight (10-15s): Why it happens

**What Works:** Real struggles, failures before wins, personal stories
**Applied To Testimonials:**
- Added: "I used to think manifestation was BS..."
- Added: "I tried everything. Nothing worked. Then..."
- Added: Screenshots and proof elements

#### 4. **App Demonstration Elements**
**What Works:** Screen recordings, real prompts, actual usage
**Applied To Educational Scripts:**
- Added screen recordings of GodGPT
- Show actual prompts being used
- Display real responses from app
- Include before/after comparisons

#### 5. **Enhanced CTAs**
**What Works:** Value-first, specific actions, engagement drivers
**Applied By Funnel:**
- **TOF:** "Comment your sign + I'll create personalized prompt"
- **MOF:** "Try this prompt and screenshot your results"
- **BOF:** "Get your reading now (Use code X for discount)"

#### 6. **Visual Consistency**
**What Works:** Consistent aesthetic, text overlays, brand recognition
**Applied To All Scripts:**
- Mystical color palette (purples, blues, golds)
- Large, bold text overlays
- App screenshots/recordings
- Split screens for comparisons
