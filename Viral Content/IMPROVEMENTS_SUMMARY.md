## ‚úÖ IMPROVEMENTS APPLIED TO ALL 32 SCRIPTS

### 1. Hook Optimization

**Before:**
- "Here's how to manifest money"
- "The 777 Manifestation Method"
- "How to Script Your Reality"

**After:**
- "You've been trying to manifest money for months. Nothing works. Here's why..."
- "You've tried the 777 method. It didn't work. Here's what you're missing..."
- "You're scripting wrong. Here's why it's not working (and how to fix it)..."

**Key Changes:**
- ‚úÖ Start with specific struggle or pain point
- ‚úÖ Use "you" language directly addressing viewer
- ‚úÖ Create curiosity gap ("Here's why...", "Here's what...")
- ‚úÖ Include emotional trigger
