## üìà KEYWORD TRACKING METRICS

### Metrics to Monitor:
- **Search Volume** - Monthly searches
- **Keyword Difficulty** - Competition level (0-100)
- **Cost Per Click (CPC)** - Average bid price
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** - Ad performance
- **Conversion Rate** - Sign-ups/purchases
- **Quality Score** - Google Ads relevance

### Target KPIs:
- **CTR:** >3% for search ads
- **Conversion Rate:** >2% for high-intent keywords
- **CPC:** <$2 for short-tail, <$1 for long-tail
- **Quality Score:** >7/10
